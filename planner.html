Job Application Strategy

To apply to 100 jobs in 20 days, you will need to be efficient and organized. Here's a roadmap to help you achieve your goals:

  1. Create a resume and cover letter template: This will allow you to quickly apply to multiple jobs without having to spend time customizing your application for each one.
  1. Look for positions on job boards: Use websites like Indeed, Glassdoor, and LinkedIn to find job postings that match your skills and interests. You can also check out company websites to see if they have any open positions.
  1. Apply to at least 5 jobs per day: Set a goal to apply to at least 5 jobs per day. This will help you reach your goal of 100 applications in 20 days.
  1. Prepare for interviews: Spend some time researching common interview questions and practicing your answers. This will help you feel more confident during interviews.
  1. Work on personal projects: Since you don't have much work experience, personal projects can help demonstrate your skills and passion for computer science. Set a goal to complete at least one project over the summer.
